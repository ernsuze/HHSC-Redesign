Fingerprinting and Eligibility for Aides

Thank you for taking this course to understand the fingerprinting and eligibility requirements necessary for serving as a certified aide in Texas.

By the end of this training, you will be equipped with the knowledge to meet compliance standards, understand your responsibilities, and confidently complete the fingerprinting process.

Before you begin, please ensure your computer meets the following requirements for a smooth course experience: Browser Compatibility: Google Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Microsoft Edge (ensure pop-ups are enabled). Printer Access: You will need access to a printer to generate a hard copy of your learning certificate upon completion. Pop-ups: Your browser must allow pop-ups to access course materials and your certificate. Note: You will be asked to confirm that you’ve completed all course requirements before receiving your certificate.
